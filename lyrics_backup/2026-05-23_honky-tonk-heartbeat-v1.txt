[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I got a honky tonk heartbeat drumming for you
Every time your shadow crosses the room
Pour a little bourbon, turn the lights down low
I want to take you where the wild wind blows

[Verse 1]
The neon is buzzing just outside the door
But I only see you moving across the floor
Your fingers are tracing the edge of my glass
We’re making a moment I know is gonna last

[Pre-Chorus]
Step a little closer to the edge of the flame
I’m burning up every time you whisper my name
The world fades away when you look at me right
So lose yourself in my arms tonight

[Chorus]
You are the rhythm in my honky tonk heartbeat
A midnight melody spinning so sweet
Wrapped in the magic of a late-night sway
I’m going to love you till the break of day

[Post-Chorus]
Oh, feel the fire pulling us deep
Listen to the sound of my honky tonk heartbeat

[Verse 2]
There’s a silver moon shining on a gravel road
And a million little secrets we’ve never told
I feel your breath like a summer breeze
Bringing this rambling heart down to its knees

[Pre-Chorus 2]
Step a little closer to the edge of the flame
I’m burning up every time you whisper my name
The world fades away when you look at me right
So lose yourself in my arms tonight

[Chorus]
You are the rhythm in my honky tonk heartbeat
A midnight melody spinning so sweet
Wrapped in the magic of a late-night sway
I’m going to love you till the break of day

[Post-Chorus 2]
Oh, feel the fire pulling us deep
Listen to the sound of my honky tonk heartbeat

[Bridge]
You are the rhythm, the fire, the light
An echo of destiny wrapped in the night
I’m falling into the silence we keep
Awakening shadows from out of the deep
Say my name softly, Saeed tonight
[Whispered] (Saeed... Saeed...)
Hold on to the feeling, our hearts taking flight
[Echo] (Saeed... Saeed...)

[Spoken]
Baby, there’s nowhere else I’d rather be
Just let the night take over, it's only you and me

[Final Chorus]
You are the rhythm in my honky tonk heartbeat
A midnight melody spinning so sweet
Wrapped in the magic of a late-night sway
I’m going to love you till the break of day
